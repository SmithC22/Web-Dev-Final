Perfect Pronunciation

What is Perfect Pronunciation?

Perfect Pronunciation is a resource for verbal practice and understanding of the English language. Users can listen to the same word said in a variety of accents and pronunciations, and attempt to parse them out.

An important part of practical language learning is listening comprehension. If you move to a new place with lots of different accents and dialects, not everyone is going to talk like the voice in Duolingo, or an in-person teacher.
This project works in an under-served area of language learning, but one that is still incredibly important.
